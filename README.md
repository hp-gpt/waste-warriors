# waste-warriors
This is a first time project involving waste management and donation, made by a few teenagers trying to make a difference :)
This website has 3 main pages [index.html, waste-donate.html, and about.html] , and 2 extra pages used for verification [verify.html, and weight-check.html].

  1. index.html - This is the home page for our project, the Waste Warriors. It includes a dashboard, explaining the process to the user, and simple tips to help our users navigate how to segregate or treat their waste before donating it.
  
  2. waste-donate.html - This is essentially the main part of our project. Here, the parent or guardian fills in the Student's name, ID number, grade and section, the type of waste they are donating, weight of the waste, the student's School House, keeping the experience easy to interact with. They are then redirected to the next page: verify.html
  
  3. verify.html - Here, the parent/guardian must go to the school to drop-off the waste, and show this verification tab to the security guard, where he will punch in the code. Upon doing so, they are redirected to the weight-check.html page.
  
  4. weight-check.html - Here, the security guard checks the weight of the waste donated and enters it in. This completes our authentication and verification process, along with the full cycle process of the waste donation for the user.
  
  5. about.html - A fun, nice way to have our users know about the contribution of each team member in the group, as well as our mission and our credits to the school.

Some other Features:

  - Mobile compatible: Our website works on all devices through the website, as our users will mainly be entering the information through their phones, and they will thus have a smooth experience.
  
  - Available on Android: While our website may not be on the Google Play Store, you can download it as an apk file on your Android phone. However, please keep in mind that the app is not yet available for Apple.
  
  - Simple Yet Effective Logging: We log all data in a simple Google Spreadsheet, to prevent overcomplication, as this is mainly for school usage, and there is no personal data being shared. Once you click the submit button, our spreadsheet immediately adds a log of the donated waste, properly sorted out.
  
  - Incentivized: We keep a track of the name, grade, class section, and school house of the student, and the weight of waste donated is converted into points,  which each student receives on every contribution. At the end of the school year, the students, classes, grades, and houses with the most added points receives recognition and a certificate for their contributions.

  - Smooth and Simple UI: Color gradients, smooth transitions, clear spacing, animations, and transitions are applied across the pages to appeal to the user aesthetically, and giving them a good experience with our website/app.
