Mother Earth is quietly suffocating, as we carelessly dispose non-degradable waste. Every item thrown without thought adds weight to a planet. Only about 9% of all plastic ever produced has been recycled globally. Most plastic ends up in landfills, incinerators, or the environment. About 93% of aluminum beverage cans sold in the UAE end up in landfills instead of being recycled. Roughly 12 million plastic containers are sold every day in the UAE, with most not recycled.

It is the duty of the youth to turn awareness into action. We are a school-led Recyclables Donation Drive organised and managed by Grade 9 students. Our aim is simple: to reduce waste, encourage recycling, and make students aware of how small actions can create a positive impact on the environment. By giving recyclable waste a second life, we reduce landfill burden and protect our planet. Every item donated is a choice to care, and a promise by students to build a cleaner future.

Designed by a team of waste-hating, landfill-allergic, slightly sleep-deprived students who care about the planet,
